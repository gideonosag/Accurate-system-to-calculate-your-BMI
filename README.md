##  calculate Body Mass Index (BMI)


# Formula

Metric: BMI = Weight(kg) / Height(m) x Height(m)




## How To Use 


**** Measure Weight in Kilograms: 70 kg
**** Measure Height in Meters: 1.75 m
*** Enter gender M or F
**** Get accurate Bmi result and category


## BMI Categories for Adults

* Underweight: Below 18.5
* Healthy Weight: 18.5 – 24.9O
* Overweight: 25.0 – 29.9
* Obesity: 30.0 or higher
